Morning Bird

Serving up our community bold fresh coffee and made in house cakes in the heart of Chicago.
Visit us for a space away from the city hustle so you can sit back and recharge with our bold coffee and perfectly paired cakes.

What's your story?

The Morning Bird team has been so humbled by the communities welcoming, that we wanted to find a way to say Thank You! While some places recognize a face and an order, we want to know our customers a little more, we want to know you. We will be hosting a meet-up the last Sunday of every month where participants can share their personal stories and proactive ways to connect with others.

And yes, they'll be tons of Morning Bird coffee and cakes!
